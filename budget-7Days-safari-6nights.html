7Days safari 6nights Tarangire-Serengeti –Ngorongoro –Lake Eyasi (hadzabe & Datoga) –Lake Manyara

Get Free Quote

Experience the ultimate short luxury safari in Tanzania, designed to give you a perfect taste of Africa’s most iconic wildlife destinations within just three unforgettable days. Beginning in Arusha, this journey takes you through the elephant-rich landscapes of Tarangire National Park and into the breathtaking Ngorongoro Crater, one of the world’s most extraordinary natural wonders. This safari combines comfort, adventure, and authenticity, allowing you to enjoy premium accommodations, expert-guided game drives, and close encounters with Africa’s famous wildlife including elephants, lions, zebras, and possibly the rare black rhino. Perfect for travelers with limited time, this itinerary delivers a complete and immersive safari experience while maintaining a high level of comfort and personalized service.

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be warmly welcomed by your professional safari guide and transferred to your luxury accommodation in Arusha. Depending on your arrival time, you will have the opportunity to relax and recover from your journey while preparing for your safari adventure. Arusha, often referred to as the safari capital of Tanzania, lies at the foothills of Mount Meru and offers a cool climate, lush surroundings, and a vibrant atmosphere. It serves as the gateway to northern Tanzania’s most famous national parks and provides a peaceful introduction to your safari experience.

Accommodation (Luxury): Gran Meliá Arusha (or similar)

Meals & Drinks: At your own expense

Day 2: Tarangire National Park

After breakfast, you will depart for Tarangire National Park, famous for its massive elephant herds and iconic baobab trees. The park is centered around the Tarangire River, the main water source attracting wildlife throughout the dry season. During your game drive, you will encounter a wide variety of animals including elephants, lions, giraffes, zebras, and over 500 bird species. A picnic lunch will be served inside the park, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the wilderness. In the late afternoon, you will continue to your luxury lodge in Karatu, located near the Ngorongoro highlands, where you can relax and enjoy the stunning landscapes.

Accommodation (Luxury): Nyota Luxury Camp (or similar)

Meals & Drinks: All meals included, drinks included (premium drinks excluded)

Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater – Return to Arusha

After an early breakfast, you will descend into the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Africa’s most remarkable wildlife destinations. The crater is a collapsed volcano that forms a natural enclosure, creating a dense concentration of wildlife within its boundaries. Inside the crater, you will experience one of the best game viewing opportunities in Africa, with sightings of wildebeest, zebras, lions, elephants, and the endangered black rhino. The diverse ecosystem includes grasslands, forests, and lakes that support an incredible variety of species. After a picnic lunch on the crater floor, you will begin your journey back to Arusha, marking the end of your unforgettable safari experience.

Accommodation: End of tour (No accommodation)

Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & Lunch included, drinks not included
