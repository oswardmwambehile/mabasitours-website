Luxury & V.I.P Tanzania Safaris

Mabasi Tours & Safaris we have a Luxury or V.I.P. safari where we accommodate our quests in 4star hotels and lodge sometime in the luxury tented camp and it may range price from $600- $850 per person per days in a full board basis. Lodges all our lodges provide full service, facilities with private rooms and bathrooms and have restaurant & lounges; some have swimming pools where you can swim during the stay. Tented also the tented camps are permanent and other are mobile they can tent according to time of the season. These provide highly comfort of a lodge within a private canvas on a solid permanents base with beds and private baths and highly quality meals.
